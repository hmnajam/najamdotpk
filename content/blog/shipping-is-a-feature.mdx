The fastest way to never ship is to treat every feature as essential. It
isn&apos;t. Most aren&apos;t.

## Decide what&apos;s v1

Write the feature list, then cross out everything that isn&apos;t needed to
prove the core idea. Be ruthless. You can always add things back once real
people are using it.

## Quality is non-negotiable, scope is

Cutting scope doesn&apos;t mean cutting corners. A small thing done well beats a
big thing done badly. Ship the small thing.
