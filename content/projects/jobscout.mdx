## Overview

JobScout turns the job hunt around: build a strong resume first, then let an
agent find the roles that actually fit. It pulls postings from across the web,
scores each one against your profile, and explains _why_ it fits — surfacing
both the reasons and the concerns — so you spend your time applying instead of
scrolling.

## What it does

- **Ranked matches, with reasons.** Run a search and review roles ordered by
  fit, each annotated with why it matches and what to watch out for.
- **Resume Studio.** Import a PDF or DOCX, refine it with AI, get ATS-style
  feedback, and export a polished version.
- **Model-agnostic, local-first.** Bring any AI provider — cloud or fully local
  — and keep your data on your own machine.

Built with Next.js and TypeScript, JobScout leans on retrieval to keep its
match explanations grounded in the real posting rather than guesswork.
